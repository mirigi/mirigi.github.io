Signature at the Sea is an exclusive residential development on Playa Brava designed by renowned Brazilian architect Marcio Kogan. Its 18 residences, 4 houses, and 3 penthouses range from 265 m² to 737 m², blending contemporary architecture with warm materials — wood, concrete, and stone — and seamless indoor-outdoor living year-round. Amenities include a semi-Olympic heated indoor pool, state-of-the-art gym with yoga and meditation areas, outdoor pool, exclusive lounge, and private terraces with built-in grills.
