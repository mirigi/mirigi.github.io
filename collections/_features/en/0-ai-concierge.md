Miri is the **first AI concierge built to act**, not just answer. Residents simply ask — *"Spaghetti Carbonara to my unit,"* or *"bring my car up in ten minutes"* — and Miri **carries the request through to completion**. No menus, no forms, no waiting on hold. **The conversation is the interface**.

For residents, Miri is connected to the building's core Mirigi modules: **restaurant ordering**, **valet parking**, **amenity reservations**, **service requests**, **visitor authorizations**, and **event sign-ups**. She works from the building's live menu, **real-time amenity availability**, and each resident's preferences, then **places the order, logs the request, and returns confirmation**. What used to require a phone call or a trip to the lobby now happens *in a sentence*.

For staff, Miri quietly **absorbs the routine traffic** that fills a front desk's day. Every action she takes flows through the **same workflows the doorman, valet, and management already use**, so nothing changes on the back end. **Inbound calls drop**, manual coordination eases, and the team is freed to focus on the **in-person hospitality** that defines a luxury building.
