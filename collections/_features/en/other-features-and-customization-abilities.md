Mirigi is **built to be tailored**. Beyond its headline modules, the platform brings together a **deep catalog of supporting capabilities** that can be activated, combined and skinned to match the **personality of each building**. Where the standard feature set falls short, **bespoke modules are quoted and developed on demand**, then delivered inside the same branded experience residents already use every day. The result is a concierge service that feels *purpose-built, not off-the-shelf*.

The platform **speaks the building's language from day one**, shipping with **English and Spanish** resident and staff interfaces and **AI-assisted translation helpers** that let staff draft notifications and content in additional locales such as Portuguese and Russian. Administrators maintain **rich Markdown content pages** — house rules, welcome guides, FAQs, building information — with **per-language versions and configurable visibility**, while a built-in **messaging center** delivers announcements and notifications with **unread tracking and real-time updates**. **Versioned terms and conditions** with explicit acceptance tracking, **AI drafting helpers** for staff communications, and **administrative reports with CSV export** round out the day-to-day operational toolkit.

Identity and reach are equally flexible. Each building can launch its own **branded mobile app**, choose from a library of **ready-made visual skins** or commission a **bespoke theme**, and extend the same UI to **lobby kiosks and concierge tablets** through a companion app with brightness and launcher-mode controls. Residents and staff are reached through **mobile push, email and SMS**, sensitive staff operations are protected by **two-factor authentication**, and a standards-based **secure API** opens the door to approved third-party integrations. **Unit groups**, finely scoped **staff roles**, a **guest access portal** and an optional **weather panel** complete a platform *engineered to bend to the building, not the other way around*.
