Governance moves faster when every unit has a clear, frictionless way to weigh in. Mirigi's **Digital Poll System** turns building-wide decisions — from lobby renovations to fee approvals — into **structured, traceable votes** that residents can answer in seconds. Votes are cast **by unit**, with optional **unit-weighted tallies** so larger stakes count proportionally alongside the standard one-unit-one-vote count. Residents participate from *in-wall panels, tablets, mobile devices, the web interface*, or simply by talking to **Miri**, the AI concierge. The result is **higher turnout**, defensible outcomes, and a durable record of every unit's response.

For staff, the system is built for **control and confidence**. Polls support two formats: **multiple choice** (single- or multi-answer, with server-enforced caps and distinctness) and **linear scale ratings** with configurable min and max. Audiences can be directed to *all units, a specific floor, an elevator line, or a hand-picked list of units*, and every poll runs on a scheduled window that moves through **draft, in course, and finished** states. Each unit's vote is **locked in once submitted**: the backend holds a per-poll lock and re-checks prior answers inside a transaction, **eliminating race conditions and double-counting**. Polls can be flagged **mandatory**, results made **public or private**, and the live dashboard renders totals and percentages with **clear column charts** — with a parallel weighted tally whenever unit-weight voting is enabled. A detailed **per-unit answer list** closes the loop for review and follow-up.

For residents, participation is **effortless and inclusive**. Ballots are **multilingual** — *title, description, and every choice* are stored per locale, so each resident reads the poll in their own language while **answer indices stay consistent** across translations. Anyone can ask **Miri** to read open polls aloud and cast their unit's vote in **natural language**; the same eligibility, locking, and **one-vote-per-unit** rules apply. The outcome is a community where **every unit is heard**, and every decision stands up to scrutiny.
