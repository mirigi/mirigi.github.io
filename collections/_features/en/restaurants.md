Mirigi turns the building itself into a **dining destination**. With the **Last Mile Shopping feature for restaurants**, residents browse full menus, place orders, and **follow each step from kitchen to door** without leaving the app. The experience is unified whether they are *dining in* at the in-building restaurant or *ordering from home* upstairs. It brings the convenience of modern food ordering into the **private, trusted environment** of the residence.

For residents, the app surfaces the **complete menu** with descriptions, pricing, dish variants, and availability, then provides **real-time status updates** from preparation through delivery. Orders can be sent to **a unit, a common area, or picked up on the way home**, removing the friction of phone calls and unclear ETAs. The same interface handles **in-building dining requests**, so a *single tap* covers both scenarios.

For restaurant staff, incoming orders arrive through a **streamlined management view** that keeps customers **automatically informed** at every stage. This reduces interruptions, cuts down on **order errors**, and lets the team concentrate on **food preparation and service quality** rather than logistics. The platform is **integration-ready** for established delivery services, so existing fulfillment workflows can continue to operate while the resident-facing experience remains consistent.

The result is **tighter operations** for the restaurant and a **more refined daily experience** for residents — a dining service that feels *native to the building* rather than bolted on.
