Mirigi's **valet parking** turns one of a building's most visible services into a **signature moment**. Residents **request their car from the phone or tablet** before they step out of the apartment, then watch the request **advance in real time** and receive a notification the instant the vehicle is waiting at the entrance. No phone calls, no curbside guessing, no awkward small talk at the door — just a quiet, *hotel-grade arrival* every time.

For residents, **every registered vehicle appears on screen** the moment it has been parked, ready to be summoned with a tap. **Visitors get the same premium treatment with zero friction**: no app, no account. As soon as their car is parked, Mirigi sends an **SMS with a personal link** and shows an on-screen **QR code** as a backup. When the visitor is ready to leave, they tap the link, scan the QR, or simply show the physical ticket, and the **valet team is notified instantly**. A second SMS goes out automatically the moment the car is staged at the ramp.

Behind the scenes, Mirigi **replaces paper logs with a clean digital workflow**. Every vehicle flows through a clear set of states — *to park, parked, requested, ready and delivered* — all visible in a **single order queue** sorted by request time. Plates are **registered once** and auto-fill unit, make, model and color from built-in catalogs. At reception and delivery, staff attach **time-stamped photos** to the vehicle's history, the definitive answer to any later **scratch or dent complaint**.

If a requested vehicle is not picked up within a **configurable window**, Mirigi automatically flips its state to *waiting to repark* and **notifies the resident** that the car has been returned to the garage. The **delivery area stays clear**, the valet always knows what to do, and management gets a **complete audit trail** of garage activity to staff peak hours correctly.
